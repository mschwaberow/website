Welcome to KinStay Massanutten

A true four-season escape in the mountains

Tucked into the wooded hillsides of Massanutten Resort, KinStay Massanutten is a warm, freshly updated house built for gathering. Two full living levels give everyone room to spread out — cozy up by the gas fireplace after a day on the slopes, cook together in the open kitchen with its live-edge dining table, or settle in downstairs for a shuffleboard tournament.

The whole home opens onto wraparound decks with rocking-chair views of the surrounding forest. With a dedicated kids' playroom, a game room, and four seasons of resort activities right outside the door, it's the kind of place families and groups come back to year after year.

  • Sleeps 10 across 4 bedrooms & 2 living areas
  • Inside Massanutten Resort — skiing, waterpark & golf
  • Game room, kids' playroom & two gas fireplaces
  • Backs up to George Washington National Forest

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

What are check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is anytime after 4:00 PM and check-out is by 10:00 AM. The home has keyless entry — we'll email your smart-lock code before you arrive, so you can head straight in.

How many people does the house sleep?

KinStay Massanutten comfortably sleeps 10 across 4 bedrooms, with two full living areas and a kids' playroom. The exact bed configuration is listed on our Airbnb and VRBO pages.

Is it good for families with young kids?

Absolutely. There's a dedicated playroom stocked with toys, plus a high chair and pack-n-play. The layout gives parents and kids room to spread out across two levels.

What's the parking and driveway like?

The driveway is a bit narrow and steep — easy to navigate by taking your time, and fine for any vehicle in normal conditions. In snow we recommend 4x4 or snow tires. There's room for several cars, plus free public parking at Painter's Pond and the ski lodge.

How far is the skiing and the waterpark?

You're inside Massanutten Resort, so the slopes, snow tubing, and the indoor/outdoor waterpark are all just minutes away.

Are pets allowed?

Small pets are welcome for a small nightly fee — just ask us first (through the request form or the listings) so we can add it to your reservation.
